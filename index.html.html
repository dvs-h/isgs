My School's Name Is INDO SCOTS GLOBAL SCHOOL

Indo Scots Global school is a co-ed, English medium school in Pune. It has a total of 43 teachers and and 29 classrooms for the students.It is affiliated to Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE) and CBSE board. It was established in 2019.The ISGS school has branches in Pune, Thane and Navi Mumbai. It provides admission from Nursery to Class 9 to the students. The school has an urban campus comprising 2 acres of land. The teacher-student ratio of the school is 1:40. It is one of the best schools in Pune. The facilities at ISGS include spacious and well ventilated smart classrooms, science and computer laboratories, library, playing area for the children, outdoor sports activities football and cricket turf for the students, transport and medical amenities, fun house for the Nursery students and CCTV cameras with fire-safety systems for the security of the students.
